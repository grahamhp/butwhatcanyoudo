Start your own

No group in your area? Start one. Here's what a local chapter does:

Coordinate poster drops

Organize group printing sessions and coordinate where posters go up across your community.

Share resources

Pool money for large-format prints, share printer access, and split costs on materials.

Build local momentum

Host meetups, canvass your neighborhood, and make sure everyone knows their rep's record before November.

How to start a chapter

1. Sign up as a volunteer

Fill out the volunteer form and select "Organize locally."

2. Recruit 2–3 people

You don't need a crowd. You need a few committed people in your area to get started.

3. Pick your first action

A poster drop is the easiest first step. Download, print, and hang them together.
