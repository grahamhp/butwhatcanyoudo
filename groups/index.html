Find people near you who are doing the work. Or start your own chapter.

What a local chapter does

You don't need permission. You don't need a budget. You need a few committed people.

Coordinate poster drops

Organize group printing sessions and coordinate where posters go up across your community.

Share resources

Pool money for large-format prints, share printer access, and split costs on materials.

Show up at events

Go to town halls and rallies together. Ask the questions that need asking. Film the answers.

How to start a chapter

1. Register below

Fill out the registration form. Your group will be listed in the directory after a quick review.

2. Set up a group chat

Facebook Groups, Discord, Signal, GroupMe — whatever works for your area. Younger organizers tend toward Discord; broader groups do well on Facebook.

3. Recruit 2–3 people

You don't need a crowd. You need a few committed people in your area to get started.

4. Pick your first action

A poster drop is the easiest first step. Download, print, and hang them together. Get posters here.
