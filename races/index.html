The proof it works

They removed their own

Thomas Massie (R-KY)

The only Republican who consistently voted against weapons packages to the government of Israel. He introduced a bill to require the foreign lobby for the government of Israel to register as a foreign agent.

Foreign lobby groups spent $9 million to defeat him. Total outside spending in his primary: $32 million.

He lost his primary on May 19, 2026 to Trump-backed Ed Gallrein (57,822 to 47,539 votes).

Both parties punish dissent on this issue.

Source: KY Secretary of State, AP, FEC filings

The people won anyway

Zohran Mamdani (D-NYC)

The Democratic establishment poured millions into backing Andrew Cuomo against Mamdani, who opposed weapons packages to the government of Israel.

Voters chose Mamdani anyway: 50.78% to Cuomo's 41.32%.

He became the first Muslim mayor of NYC, the first of South Asian heritage, and the youngest mayor in over a century. Took office January 1, 2026.

When enough people show up, they win.

Source: NYC Board of Elections, AP
