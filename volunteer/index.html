Get Involved

Stop asking what you can do. Start doing it.

Ways you can help

Print & hang posters

Download posters for your district. Print them at home or at a shop. Hang them where people will see them.

Organize locally

Start or join a local group in your area. Coordinate poster drops, share resources, and keep each other motivated.

Create content

Good at social media, video, writing, or design? Help create shareable content that spreads the message.

Research

Help track lobbying donations, voting records, and candidate information. We need people who are good with data.

Spread the word

Talk to people. Share the site. Post the link. Sometimes the simplest thing is the most powerful.

Something else

Have a skill or idea we haven't thought of? We want to hear from you. Every campaign needs surprises.

Sign up

Fill out the form below and we'll reach out with next steps. No spam. No selling your info. Just the work.

Not ready to volunteer?

That's okay. You can still help right now by downloading a poster or sharing the site.
