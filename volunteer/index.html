This needs to be together.

To grow, we need people in specific roles. If one of these sounds like you, we need you now — not later.

Content Creators

Video editors, TikTok/Reels makers, graphic designers, copywriters. People who can make content that stops the scroll and gets shared. Short-form video is how this goes viral.

Social Media Managers

People who can run accounts on Instagram, TikTok, X, Threads, or Bluesky. Post consistently, engage with comments, build the audience. Multiple managers across platforms is the goal.

Researchers

Track voting records, lobbying money, candidate statements, and election data. Verify facts. Keep the database accurate. If you're good with data and care about getting things right, this is you.

Rally & Event Attendees

Go to town halls, candidate events, and political rallies. Ask the questions that need asking — on camera if possible. Polite, direct, impossible to ignore. We need people in every district who are willing to show up.

Volunteer Coordinators

Help organize other volunteers in your state or city. Manage local groups, coordinate poster drops, and keep people connected and active. The glue that holds the rest of it together.

Print & Distribution

Print posters and hang them. Know a print shop willing to print at cost? Even better. Businesses willing to hang a poster in their window? That's a win.

Something else entirely

Developers, translators, lawyers, organizers, teachers, musicians — if you have a skill and a conscience, there's a way to use it. Tell us what you're good at.
