Where the money goes

This campaign has zero budget. It was built by two people on nights and weekends. Every dollar donated goes directly to:

Hosting & tools

The website, domain, and production tools like Canva Pro that keep content going out the door.

Printing

Subsidizing poster printing in swing districts so volunteers can put them up without paying out of pocket.

Targeted outreach

Instagram and social media ads in key districts, video and content production to share widely.

Other ways to support

Money helps, but it's not the only way. Here are free ways to support the campaign:

  • Share the site — Send the link to three people. That's it.
  • Print a poster — Download one and hang it up.
  • Post on social media — Use #ButWhatCanYouDo and #WhoVotedYes
  • Volunteer your time — Sign up and help where it's needed.
  • Talk to people — The most powerful tool in any campaign is a conversation.
