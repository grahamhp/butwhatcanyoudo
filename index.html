It's already working

Mamdani beat Cuomo. AIPAC-backed candidates lost.

When people show up, the money loses. In New York, AIPAC spent millions — and their candidates still lost. This isn't a fantasy. It's happening.

Thomas Massie — one of the only Republicans who voted against weapons to the government of Israel — was primaried out by his own party.

Both parties punish dissent on this issue. That's why this isn't about parties. It's about people.

Not left. Not right.
Just human.

This campaign is not pro-Democrat or pro-Republican. It's not pro-any-party. It is for every American who believes that their tax dollars should not fund the killing of civilians — and who wants to do something about it.

If there's one issue that can bring us together, saying no to genocide should be it.
